  • About Treasure Island

    Let's say you have a small fortune
    of some stocks, a flat or house, several dozen ounces of physical gold, a few hundred ounces of physical silver, as well as two plots of land, a car and a certain amount of several cryptocurrencies, and of course some cash reserves in several currencies.

    It's hard to say how much that's worth at any given time,
    because obviously the value of your wealth changes over time during, say, a corona-crash, a bull market in cryptocurrencies, or weakness in the US dollar.

    In addition, you have domestic and foreign stocks in a total of three brokerage accounts, some bullion buried under your favourite oak tree by the lake, some you have in a safe. Similarly, you have cash in several bank accounts, some in banknotes.

    In such a situation it is difficult to make a quick assessment of the value of your assets.
    This is where Treasure Island can help.

    Treasure Island is a tool to monitor the value of your wealth consisting of various assets.

    In Treasure Island you can add to your portfolio more than 14 000 different assets:

    • Shares and ETFs listed on the US Stock Exchange
    • Shares and bonds listed on the Warsaw Stock Exchange
    • Cryptocurrencies
    • Commodities
    • Gold and silver bullion
    • Cash in more than 30 currencies

    You can also add other assets - such as real estate, cars, numismatic items or works of art, the value of which you can enter yourself, but it is not constantly monitored.

    With TI it is possible to track the current* value of your portfolio and each of its components in over 30 currencies, as well as monitor the percentage of each component. TI also allows you to download a portfolio report in .xlsx file.
